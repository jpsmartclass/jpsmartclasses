About Our Primary Classes (1–5)

At JP Smart Classes, we believe that a child’s learning journey begins with strong foundations. For Classes 1 to 5, we provide interactive online tuition for English, Math, EVS, and Hindi aligned with both CBSE and State Board curriculums. Our lessons are designed to make learning joyful, while ensuring long-term retention.

Subjects We Cover

  • English: Reading comprehension, grammar, writing skills, and storytelling.
  • Math: Number sense, operations, geometry, and fun problem-solving activities.
  • EVS: Environmental awareness, science basics, and social studies concepts.
  • Hindi: Vocabulary building, grammar, reading practice, and creative writing.

Why Parents Choose JP Smart Classes

  • Concept-first learning with 2D animations and visual aids.
  • Engaging worksheets and interactive activities.
  • Regular parent–teacher updates to track progress.
  • Small batch size for personalized attention.
  • Both live and recorded classes for flexible learning.
