About Our Secondary Coaching

At JP Smart Classes, we specialize in preparing students of Classes 9 to 12 for CBSE and State Boards. Our teaching focuses on building strong concepts in Physics, Chemistry, Math, Biology, English, and Commerce (Accounts & Economics). We ensure that students not only perform well in board exams but also develop skills useful for competitive exams.

Subjects We Cover

  • Physics & Chemistry: Concept clarity with experiments, diagrams, and derivations.
  • Mathematics: NCERT coverage, problem-solving shortcuts, and board-level practice.
  • Biology: Diagrams, flowcharts, and detailed explanations for quick recall.
  • English: Literature, grammar, comprehension, and answer-writing strategies.
  • Accounts & Economics: Step-by-step problem solving and case studies.
